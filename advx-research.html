⚠️ Controversy Notes争议档案

Dreamer Database — data sold to sponsorsDreamer Database — 数据出售给赞助商

Ex-organizer "Max" exposed (2025-07) that applicant/visitor data was compiled into a "Dreamer Database" and sponsor access sold for ~¥90k, with 4 alleged PIPL violations (no valid separate consent, illegal cross-border transfer, over-collection incl. rejected applicants). Official response attacked the whistleblower instead of the problem. 前组织成员 Max（2025-07）揭露：参赛者/访客数据被汇编为「梦想家数据库」，向赞助商出售访问权约 9 万元；涉 4 项 PIPL 违规（无有效单独同意、非法跨境传输、过度收集含落选者数据）。官方回应选择攻击揭露者而非解决问题。

Hong Kong entity — PIPL sidestep attempt香港主体 — 规避 PIPL 的尝试

AdventureX Education Technology Ltd registered in HK 2025-11-14 (#79171672); the 2026 policy claims HK PDPO only + exclusive HK jurisdiction. Legal analysis (SmallWOD): PIPL still applies — the event runs in Hangzhou and serves mainland residents (PIPL Art. 3); choice-of-law clauses can't exclude public law. 逃逸計畫教育科技有限公司 2025-11-14 在港注册（编号 79171672）；2026 政策声称仅适用香港 PDPO + 香港专属管辖。法律分析（SmallWOD）：PIPL 依然适用——活动在杭州举办、服务大陆居民（PIPL 第 3 条）；合同选法条款无法排除公法。

SmallWOD lawsuit — AdvX sued its critic and lostSmallWOD 诉讼 — 起诉批评者并败诉

AdventureX sued the author of the privacy-compliance critique for defamation. The court ruled for the defendant: critical analysis of privacy practices is protected speech — a notable precedent for tech criticism in China. AdventureX 以名誉权侵权起诉隐私合规批判文章作者「虾仁芝麻卷」。法院判被告胜诉：对隐私实践的批判性分析属受保护言论——中国科技批评的重要先例。
